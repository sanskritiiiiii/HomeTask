He’s always MS Dhoni’s first pick of man to go and pick up wickets under pressure: AB de Villiers on Moeen Ali

Posted on Apr 04, 2023

South African star batter AB de Villiers praised the Chennai Super King off-spinner Moen Ali after his Player of the Match performance against Lucknow Super Giants on Monday and said Ali’s wicket-taking ability under pressure will make him the go-to man for skipper MS Dhoni. Speaking on Jio Cinema de Villiers said, “He’s always Dhoni’s first pick of man to go and pick up wickets under pressure. I thought he should’ve bowled a little earlier but MS the master and the guru, he always knows better.”
